What are Crypto Currencies?

Crypto Currencies are digital tokens, which allow people to make payments directly to each other through an online system. The records are also maintained by a decentralized system using cryptography, rather than by a centralized authority. Crypto Currencies also contain real money, which can be traded on blockchain technology. The top 3 popular Crypto Currencies are the following: Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), and Tether (USDT). These were ranked by the people who mostly used them. Although Crypto Currencies have a lot of great uses like transferring funds from one place to another, always remember that some Crypto Currencies are scams, so you should always be careful if you plan to engage in things like these.
